How it works

At Still Force Consults, we prioritize your safety and peace of mind by offering top-notch security solutions tailored to your needs. Here’s how we make it simple for you:

  • Contact Us: Reach out to us via phone, email, or our website’s contact form. Let us know your security needs and concerns.
  • Initial Consultation: Our team will schedule a consultation to understand your requirements in detail. This can be done in person or remotely, depending on your preference.
  • Site Assessment (if applicable): For services like CCTV installation, access control, or technical surveillance countermeasures, we’ll conduct a thorough on-site assessment to identify potential vulnerabilities.
  • Customized Solutions: Based on your needs, we’ll provide a tailored quote outlining the services, equipment, and timeline.
  • Implementation: Once approved, our professional team gets to work, ensuring high-quality and timely execution of the project.
  • Follow-Up Support: Post-installation or service, we offer ongoing support, maintenance, and consultancy to ensure your security systems remain effective.
